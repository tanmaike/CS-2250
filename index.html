Soft Chocolate Sugar Cookies

These Sugar Cookies are a party of favority of many, with a rich flavor that's complemented by a light sweetness. I've made these treats dozens of times, and every time, they're gone within the hour.

These cookies are highly reliant on its main ingredient: cocoa powder. It's best to not concede to using a cheaper cocoa. The richer the cocoa, the deeper the flavor. I personally recommend using King Arthur Baking Company's Black Cocoa. Its wonderful flavor is made even more wonderful by its fair price.
